What PurgeQ is

PurgeQ is a community browser extension that lets you keep a personal list of FACEIT players you don't want to queue with, and optionally share that list with friends. The service is operated by an independent maintainer (the "operator", see contact below) and is not affiliated with FACEIT or its parent company.

Eligibility

You must be at least 13 years old to use PurgeQ — the same minimum age FACEIT requires. If you live in a jurisdiction that sets a higher digital-consent age (for example 16 in some EU countries), you must meet that age or have parental authorisation.

Your account

  • One account per Discord identity.
  • Don't impersonate another person, share your account, or sell access to it.
  • You're responsible for what's stored under your account, including the contents of bans and the people you invite to your banlist.

You're responsible for what you store. If a ban entry contains defamatory content or personal data you weren't entitled to publish, the legal exposure is yours.

Service availability

PurgeQ is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. The service runs on Supabase + Cloudflare; outages, planned maintenance or data loss can happen. There's no SLA. We recommend periodically exporting your data via Settings → Your data → Export my data.

Termination

You can delete your account at any time via Settings → Your data → Delete account. The deletion is immediate and irreversible — it removes your profile, banlist, every ban you authored, your invites and memberships.

We can terminate or suspend access for serious violations of these terms or applicable law. We'll try to notify you when possible.
