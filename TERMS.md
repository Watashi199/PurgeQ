# PurgeQ — Terms of Service

_Last updated: 2026-05-18_

By signing in to PurgeQ you agree to these terms. They're intentionally
short — read them once and you're done.

## What PurgeQ is

PurgeQ is a community browser extension that lets you keep a personal
list of FACEIT players you don't want to queue with, and optionally
share that list with friends. The service is operated by an
independent maintainer (the "operator", see contact below) and is
**not affiliated with FACEIT** or its parent company.

## Eligibility

You must be at least 13 years old to use PurgeQ — the same minimum
age FACEIT requires. If you live in a jurisdiction that sets a higher
digital-consent age (for example 16 in some EU countries), you must
meet that age or have parental authorisation.

## Your account

- You sign in with Discord. We rely on Discord's identity for who
  you are; we don't store a password.
- One account per Discord identity.
- Don't impersonate another person, share your account, or sell
  access to it.
- You're responsible for what's stored under your account, including
  the contents of bans and the people you invite to your banlist.

## Acceptable use

You agree **not** to:

- Use PurgeQ to harass, threaten, defame or stalk other players.
- Publish reasons that contain personal data, hate speech, or content
  that's illegal where you live.
- Mass-import lists you don't have the right to redistribute (e.g.
  scraped from other communities) without their consent.
- Try to bypass the rate limits, RLS policies, or bot-protection
  challenge.
- Reverse-engineer the SaaS backend with the intent of disrupting it.

We may suspend or delete an account that violates these rules — we
will do so manually and only with cause.

## User-generated content

When you create a ban entry, you grant PurgeQ a non-exclusive licence
to store, replicate and show that entry **only to you and to people
you explicitly invite to your banlist**. We never publish your bans
publicly, sell them, or use them to train models.

You're responsible for what you store. If a ban entry contains
defamatory content or personal data you weren't entitled to publish,
the legal exposure is yours.

## Service availability

PurgeQ is provided **as-is**, without warranty of any kind. The
service runs on Supabase + Cloudflare; outages, planned maintenance or
data loss can happen. There's no SLA. We recommend periodically
exporting your data via **Settings → Your data → Export my data**.

## Pricing

PurgeQ is free to use. If a paid tier ever launches, the free tier
will keep working with reasonable limits, and you'll be notified in
advance.

## Termination

You can delete your account at any time via **Settings → Your data
→ Delete account**. The deletion is immediate and irreversible — it
removes your profile, banlist, every ban you authored, your invites
and memberships.

We can terminate or suspend access for serious violations of these
terms or applicable law. We'll try to notify you when possible.
